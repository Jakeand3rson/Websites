About Roger

Roger Anderson has a God-given talent for troubleshooting along with an innate ability to tackle any area of home repair, maintenance and remodeling. As the son of a machinist and carpenter, and the grandson of a blacksmith and a farmer, trouble shooting and repairing come second nature to him. With a degree in electronics, he worked in the computer industry for 20 years. After talking with many of his elderly friends and hearing how often they had been taken advantage of by unscrupulous contractors, he left the computer world and began Roger’s Repair to be the change in the industry.

As a licensed, bonded and insured General Contractor and Handyman in the State of Washington, Roger works for home owners, property investors, property managers, in-state and out-of-state landlords and commercial retailers. He has a long list of clients who have offered to give testimonies of his work and character, so please ask if you’re interested, so please don't hesitate to ask. We encourage all our new clients to visit the Labor & Industry site to verify license, bond and insurance, and also for any past complaints no matter who you hire.
