About Roger

Roger Anderson has a natural talent for troubleshooting along with an innate ability to tackle all areas of home repair, maintenance and remodeling. As the son of a machinist and carpenter, and the grandson of a blacksmith and a farmer, trouble shooting and repairs come second nature to him. With a degree in electronics, he worked in the computer industry for 20 years. After talking with amny of his elderly friends and hearing how often they had been taen advantage of by unscrupulous contractors, he left the computer world and began Roger’s Repair to be the change in the industry.

A a licensed, bonded and insured general contractor in the state of Washington, Roger has worked form home owners of all ages, property rehabbers, property managers and commercial retailers. He has a long list of clients who have offered to give testimonies of his work and character. So please ask if you’re interested.
